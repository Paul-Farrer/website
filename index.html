About Me

My name is Paul Farrer and I am currently based in the UK. I recently graduated with a BSC honours degree in computer science and games development.

While playing computer games in childhood sparked my initial interest, over the years, this grew into a passion which required me to understand how the actual games I played were created and developed. Further research subsequently led me to discover my first game engine; DarkBasic Professional, and from then, I understood that I wanted to work within the computer video games industry.

Although I frequently spend time on many different elements of video game creation, such as, 2D & 3D modelling/sculpting, programming the functionality and animation. I have also produced guides on Steam involving the modification of game files. One such guide has amassed more than 4000 visitors.
